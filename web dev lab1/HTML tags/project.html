Pizza pepperoni

  • Time:25
  • Serves:8
Ingredients Quantity
Pizza dough 16 ounces
Pizza sauce 1/2 cup
Pepperoni 18-20 slices
Mozarella cheese 12 ounces
Fresh oregano 1 teaspoon

Step 1: Preheat oven to 500°F.
Step 2: Roll out dough on a lightly floured surface.
If it's hard to roll, let it rest for 5 minutes so
it can come to room temperature.
Step 3: Transfer the dough to a lightly dusted pizza
peel. Alternatively, fit it into a large cast-iron.
Step 4: Add sauce in a light layer all over the
pizza, leaving about 1/4-inch crust around the edges. Chop half
of the pepperoni and sprinkle it over the sauce. Top the pizza with
grated cheese and the rest of the pepperoni.
Step 5: Transfer pizza to a 500 ̊F oven and bake for
10 to 12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.
